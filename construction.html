Corral & Loading Chute Rebuild — Finished

January 2025  ·  MYT Enterprises Construction Team

The old corral system was well past its prime — rotted posts, broken boards, and a loading chute that had become more of a hazard than a help. Over the course of three weeks, we stripped it all out and put in a brand new system: steel pipe corral panels, heavy-duty headgate, and a rebuilt loading chute with solid footings.

The result is a safer, faster working environment for the cattle operation. We're already seeing the difference at processing time.

Build Tip

Why We Choose Post-Frame for Agricultural Buildings

November 2024  ·  MYT Enterprises Construction Team

Post-frame construction (commonly called pole barn construction) has been the go-to method for agricultural buildings for decades — and for good reason. It's cost-effective, versatile, and can be built quickly compared to traditional stick or metal stud framing.

Key advantages include wider clear-span openings (great for equipment storage), faster build times, and easy future expansion. When done right with proper drainage and site prep, a post-frame building will last generations.
